## Purpose

The SIGNAL chatbot provides conversational access to enriched PBH social listening data, enabling pharma marketers to:
- Query patient sentiment and experiences
- Explore treatment discussions
- Identify emerging trends
- Access high-impact posts

---

## Data Source

The chatbot operates on the enriched dataset produced by the v5 enrichment system:
- 43 enrichment fields per post
- Dictionary-based entity extraction
- AI-powered classification
- Adverse event and crisis detection
- Platforms: Reddit, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram
